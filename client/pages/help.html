FAQ

Where can I see my balance?
You can see your balance by logging into your account and visiting the 'Profile' page. You can also see your balance in the navbar, in the top right corner of the page.
Do I need an account to place an order or deliver?
Yes, you need to have an account to place an order or deliver items.
Is it possible to change the order after it is confirmed?
It is possible to change or delete the order as long as it is in the state "Pending". You can edit the order in the "My Orders" page of your account. However, when the order is "Picked Up", it is no longer possible to change or delete the order.
Where can I follow the status of my order?
You can track your order status in the 'My Orders' page of your account.
As a deliverer, how can I contact the customer?
As a deliverer, you can find contact information and info about the order in the "My Deliveries" page of your account.
As a customer, how can I contact the deliverer?
As a customer, you can find contact information to the deliverer under "My Orders".
