Our Terms of Use

Some simple, but very important rules to remember!

  • You must have turned 16 to order and to deliver.
  • We are regularly collecting data according to our policy, to create a more valuable experience for our users.
  • If you do not follow our terms of use, you can be cancelled from KaffeKuriren.
  • This is a service for Linköping University's students and teachers! Therefore, you need to be associated with Linköping University to use KaffeKuriren.
  • To put an order, you pay an extra fee of 7 SEK for each item which is the reward to your deliverer - a coffee as thanks!
  • This is a system where students can help each other. If something however would happen to your order or when you deliver, the cost is shared between both parties. If there are any additional problems, feel free to send us an email and we can solve it together!
