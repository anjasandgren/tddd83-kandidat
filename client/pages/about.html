About KaffeKuriren

From coffeedrinkers to coffeedrinkers

Welcome to KaffeKuriren – Linköping University’s very own peer-to-peer coffee delivery service! Founded in 2025 by a group of passionate students out of a simple but powerful idea: students helping students staying caffeinated since they don’t always have the time (or energy) to grab the caffeine they needed.

Our mission is to fuel focus, foster connection, and make great coffee more accessible for everyone at Linköpings University. Because let’s face it, everything is better with coffee. ☕
